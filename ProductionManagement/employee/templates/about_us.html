CORAL TEXTILES India Pvt Ltd.

About Us

At CORAL TEXTILES, we understand that quality begins with the fabric. That’s why we have invested in advanced knitting technology to ensure that every meter of fabric we produce meets the highest standards. Our team of skilled professionals, combined with the latest machinery, allows us to deliver superior products that set us apart in the competitive textile market.

Our Expertise

Our expertise lies in the manufacturing of circular-knitted fabrics. We offer an extensive range of materials, including single jersey, double jersey, rib, and interlock fabrics. Whether you are looking for lightweight, breathable fabrics for T-shirts or stretchable, durable materials for sportswear, we have the solution for you. Our products are known for their smooth finish, vibrant colors, and long-lasting quality, which makes them ideal for a variety of applications.

We take pride in our ability to customize fabrics to meet the specific needs of our clients. With our advanced Jacquard knitting machines, we can create intricate patterns and designs that add a unique touch to your products. From simple textures to complex designs, our machines are capable of producing a variety of knit fabrics that cater to both basic and high-end market demands.

State-of-the-Art Technology

At the heart of CORAL TEXTILES is our cutting-edge machinery. Our range of Mayer & Cie knitting machines is designed to deliver precise, consistent, and high-quality output. These machines are renowned for their efficiency and versatility, enabling us to meet diverse fabric requirements without compromising on quality. We continuously update our equipment and processes to keep up with the latest advancements in textile technology, ensuring that we remain at the forefront of the industry.

Commitment to Quality and Sustainability

Quality is the foundation of everything we do at CORAL TEXTILES. From sourcing raw materials to the final inspection, we have stringent quality control measures in place to ensure that our fabrics meet the highest standards. Our team of experts carefully monitors every stage of the production process, ensuring that each fabric roll is flawless and ready for use.

We are also committed to sustainability. In today’s world, it’s essential to adopt eco-friendly practices, and at CORAL TEXTILES, we are dedicated to reducing our environmental footprint. Our manufacturing processes are designed to minimize waste, and we prioritize the use of sustainable raw materials whenever possible. By doing so, we not only protect our planet but also provide our customers with fabrics that are responsibly made.

Why Choose Us?

Choosing CORAL TEXTILES means partnering with a company that values quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction. We offer:

  • Superior Quality: Our fabrics are made to last, ensuring durability and comfort.
  • Customization: Tailored fabric solutions to meet your specific needs.
  • Advanced Technology: Use of the latest knitting machines for precise and consistent results.
  • Sustainability: Eco-friendly practices that align with global environmental standards.

Thank you for choosing CORAL TEXTILES. We look forward to building long-lasting partnerships and providing you with the best quality fabrics. For more information, feel free to contact us or explore our product range.
